BeesWax Wraps











Sustaina is an eco-friendly and sustainable beeswax wrap company. Our wraps are handmade with organic cotton, beeswax, jojoba oil, and tree resin, making them breathable, reusable, and compostable. Our modern and stylish designs come in a variety of sizes, perfect for wrapping sandwiches, covering leftovers, and keeping fruits and vegetables fresh. We are committed to sustainability, quality, and community. Choose Sustaina for an eco-friendly alternative to single-use plastics.
